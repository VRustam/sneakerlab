# 3D asset attribution

`materials-variants-shoe-1024.glb` is a web-optimized version of **Materials
Variants Shoe**, sourced from the
[KhronosGroup glTF Sample Assets](https://github.com/KhronosGroup/glTF-Sample-Assets/tree/main/Models/MaterialsVariantsShoe).

- Model: Loic Norgeot / Shopify, 2021
- Additional real-time refraction work: Sketchfab, 2019
- License: [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

The asset is used here as a local interactive 3D commerce demonstration. It is not
represented as a SneakerLab-owned product model.
